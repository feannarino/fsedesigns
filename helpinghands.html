BRANDING

HELPING HANDS

Helping Hands is a theoretical smartphone app dedicated to helping opiate addicts and families of addicts find resources to help them recover, such as therapists and anonymous meetings, as well as a semi-anonymous social networking platform to connect and find support within the community.

This brand is focused on positivity. People in addiction recovery face many challenges and stigma, and those who use Helping Hands should feel safe and happy. I used soft imagery and cool colors to evoke feelings of peace and serenity.
